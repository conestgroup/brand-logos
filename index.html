Brand Strategy

Key decisions before picking a name

✓ Keep separate from BananaSplit

The staffing company serves warehouse employers (B2B). Linking it visually to BananaSplit would signal "sober living housing" to corporate HR — which may create hesitation before we've proven our workforce quality. Operational synergy yes, brand connection no.

→ Our workforce story is a strength

People who've overcome incarceration or addiction are motivated, loyal, and grateful for opportunity. The brand name should work for employers without triggering stigma, while the internal culture celebrates the mission.

⚠ Watch out for "Freedom"

Freedom Staffing is emotionally powerful for workers — but B2B buyers may immediately read "formerly incarcerated." Best used for internal culture and worker recruitment, not as the primary employer-facing brand name.
