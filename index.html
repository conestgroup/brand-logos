Brand Strategy

Key decisions before we pick a name

✓ Keep it separate from BananaSplit

The staffing company serves warehouse employers (B2B). Linking it visually to BananaSplit would signal "housing for people in recovery" — which may create hesitation among corporate clients before we've proven the quality of our workforce.

→ Our workforce story

We place people who've overcome incarceration, addiction, or other challenges. This is a strength — they're motivated, loyal, and grateful for opportunity. The brand name should work for employers without triggering stigma, while the internal culture celebrates the mission.

⚠ Watch out for "Freedom"

Freedom Staffing is emotionally powerful for workers — but B2B buyers may immediately read "formerly incarcerated." Recommended use: internal messaging and community outreach only, not as the primary brand name for employer-facing materials.
