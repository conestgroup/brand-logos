Six More Brand Concepts

Each name was selected with our Phoenix warehouse staffing niche in mind — and how it pairs with the BananaSplit ecosystem of housing + workforce.

Strong B2B · Trust Signal

Cornerstone Staffing

A cornerstone is the first stone laid — everything is built from it. For our workers, we are the cornerstone of their new career. For employers, we're the cornerstone of their labor supply chain. Immediately projects stability and reliability to corporate clients.

Synergy: pairs naturally with BananaSplit — "we provide the housing cornerstone, you provide the career cornerstone."

A bridge connects two sides — we connect people to opportunity. "Bridgeway" implies a path, a route, a way through. Subtle but powerful for our mission. Also connects to "Bridge to Compassion" already in the BananaSplit partner ecosystem, creating a natural thematic family.

Ecosystem note: BananaSplit works with Bridge to Compassion (BTC). "Bridgeway" creates a thematic family: Bridge → Bridgeway → BananaSplit.

Summit Workforce

Summit means the highest point — aspirational for workers climbing back, credible for employers who want peak performance. Clean, one-word identity that works nationally. The mountain silhouette creates instant visual recognition and works beautifully on uniforms and hard hats.

Positioning: "We help people reach their Summit." Tagline writes itself.

Modern · Tech-Forward · Scalable

Nexus Staffing Group

Nexus means a connection or link — we're the nexus between employer and worker. It sounds modern and technology-forward, which helps with larger corporate clients and ATS systems. Strong for a company that plans to scale and use tech (which we are — portal, smart locks, automation).

Tech angle: aligns with our automation-first approach across BananaSplit and CoNest. "The connected workforce."

Reliability · Employer Favorite

Steadfast Workforce

Steadfast means unwavering, loyal, committed — exactly what warehouse employers need to hear. High no-show rates are the #1 pain point in industrial staffing. This name directly answers that fear before the sales conversation even begins. Particularly powerful for Amazon, UPS, and large logistics clients.

Sales angle: "Our workers don't no-show. That's what Steadfast means." Strongest name for closing corporate B2B contracts.

Bold · Western · Phoenix Spirit

Trailwork Staffing

A portmanteau of "trailblazer" and "work" — we blaze the trail to employment. Has a distinctly Western, Phoenix spirit that competitors from outside the market can't easily copy. Warm amber tones connect to the Arizona desert aesthetic. Memorable and unique — no other staffing company is using this name.

Local angle: deeply Phoenix. The desert trail metaphor resonates with Arizona culture and makes the brand feel genuinely local, not corporate transplant.
